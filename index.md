# 122 Free AI Prompts for Transcript Processing

A curated library of 122 prompts for ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other AI chat tools. Each prompt is designed to transform raw speaker-labeled transcripts into business-ready output — summaries, blog posts, legal memos, social content, and more.

These prompts pair naturally with [AI transcription with speaker identification](https://brasstranscripts.com/?utm_source=github-pages&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=prompts-library), but they work with any transcript that has Speaker A / Speaker B / etc. labels.

27 prompts for meeting summaries, decision logs, action-item extraction, board-pack prep, and strategic analysis.

### [Content Marketing](prompts/markdown/content-marketing/)
25 prompts for turning podcasts and interviews into blog posts, social suites, lead magnets, newsletters, and SEO-ready copy.

### [Legal Professional](prompts/markdown/legal-professional/)

### [Freelancer & General Content](prompts/markdown/general-content/)
59 prompts for general-purpose transcript cleanup, formatting, translation prep, and creative repurposing.

## Quick Start

1. **Get a clean transcript with speaker labels.** Any transcript with `Speaker A:` / `Speaker B:` markers works.
2. **Pick a prompt** from a category above that matches your goal.
3. **Paste the prompt + your transcript** into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or your preferred AI chat tool.
4. **Iterate.** Most prompts have variations — adjust the parameters in `[brackets]` to match your tone, length, and audience.

Every prompt is plain Markdown — copy, fork, remix freely under the MIT license.
