**Category**: Legal Professional
**Difficulty**: Intermediate
**Estimated Tokens**: 900-1300

## Description

Transform client consultation transcripts into comprehensive intake summaries for case file documentation. Captures factual background, potential claims, damages, and client expectations in a structured format for case evaluation.

**Important**: AI transcription is intended for internal case preparation and reference purposes only. Attorneys should verify all client-provided information and maintain appropriate documentation standards.

## Best Practices

**Capture client's words:** Preserve client's own descriptions and statements for accurate documentation.

**Identify statute of limitations:** Note any time-sensitive deadlines that could affect case viability.

**Document all parties:** Record names of all potential defendants, witnesses, and other involved parties.

**Note evidentiary needs:** Identify documents and evidence the client should preserve or obtain.

## Use Cases

- **Initial consultations** - Create comprehensive intake records from first client meetings
- **Case evaluation** - Document information needed to assess case merit and complexity
- **Retainer documentation** - Record fee discussions and scope of representation
- **Referral preparation** - Document cases for potential referral to other attorneys
- **Conflict checking** - Identify all parties for conflict of interest review
