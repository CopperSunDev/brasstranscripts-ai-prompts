# Performance Review Documentation Helper

**Category**: Executive Business
**Difficulty**: Advanced
**Estimated Tokens**: 800-1200
**Version**: 1.0.0

## Description

Extract performance-related discussions and contributions for employee development and review processes. This prompt identifies achievements, growth areas, and development opportunities from meeting conversations.

## Best Practices

**Objective documentation:** Focus on specific behaviors and contributions rather than subjective interpretations.

**Development-focused:** Frame observations in terms of growth opportunities rather than criticisms.

**Recognition balance:** Identify both achievements and areas for improvement for comprehensive development planning.

**Confidentiality awareness:** Use this information appropriately for constructive performance discussions.

## Use Cases

- **Annual performance reviews** - Gather year-round evidence of contributions and growth
- **Development planning** - Identify specific areas for skill building and training
- **Recognition programs** - Document achievements worthy of formal recognition
- **Team optimization** - Understand collaboration patterns and working relationships
- **Succession planning** - Identify leadership potential and development readiness
- **Goal setting** - Align individual aspirations with business needs

## Advanced Techniques

**360-degree perspective integration:** Combine insights across meetings:
```text
For comprehensive performance documentation:
- Track individual contributions across multiple meetings over time
- Note consistency of performance patterns and development progress
- Identify trends in collaboration effectiveness and skill demonstration
- Document evolution of responsibilities and growth in capabilities
```

**Development pathway mapping:** Connect observations to career planning:
```text
For each individual, consider:
- Current role performance versus expectations
- Demonstrated readiness for increased responsibility
- Skill gaps that limit advancement opportunities
- Natural strengths that could be leveraged in new roles
```

## Integration Tips

**HR system integration:** Export insights to performance management systems for formal review processes.

**Development planning:** Use observations to create specific, actionable development plans.

**Regular tracking:** Document performance insights from ongoing meetings to build comprehensive review data.
