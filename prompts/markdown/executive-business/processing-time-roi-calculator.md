## Best Practices

**Include all cost factors:** Account for direct costs, opportunity costs, and staff time to get accurate ROI picture.

**Quantify time savings:** Convert hours saved into dollar values using appropriate hourly rates.

**Consider workflow acceleration:** Fast transcription enables same-day workflows that weren't possible before.

**Track actual metrics:** Use real data from your organization rather than industry estimates.
