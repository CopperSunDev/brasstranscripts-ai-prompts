# Professional Follow-Up Email Creator

**Category**: General Content
**Difficulty**: Beginner
**Estimated Tokens**: 400-600
**Version**: 1.0.0

## Description

Generate polished client emails that demonstrate professionalism and ensure alignment. This prompt transforms call discussions into well-structured follow-up emails that build confidence and maintain project momentum.

## The Prompt

```text
Create a professional follow-up email for my client based on this call transcript.

TRANSCRIPT: [Paste your client call transcript here]

Please write a concise, professional email that includes:

## Email Structure:
- Professional subject line
- Warm opening acknowledging the conversation
- Key decisions summary (3-5 bullet points maximum)
- Next steps with clear timelines
- Professional closing with contact information

## Tone Guidelines:
- Confident and professional
- Collaborative and positive
- Clear and specific
- Grateful for their time

## Key Elements to Include:
- Main project decisions made
- Timeline confirmations
- My immediate next steps
- What I need from them
- Next meeting/check-in scheduling
```

## Best Practices

**Timing matters:** Send follow-up emails within 24 hours while the conversation is fresh.

**Subject line clarity:** Make the subject specific to the project and conversation topic.

**Action items separation:** Clearly distinguish between what you'll do and what they need to do.

**Professional tone:** Maintain confidence without being pushy or overly casual.

## Use Cases

- **Discovery call follow-up** - Summarize initial project discussions and next steps
- **Progress meeting recap** - Confirm status updates and new decisions
- **Scope discussion summary** - Document changes and their implications
- **Timeline adjustment confirmation** - Acknowledge delays and revised schedules
- **Milestone completion notification** - Update clients on deliverable status
- **Problem resolution recap** - Confirm solutions and prevention measures
