# Funny Commentary Extractor

**Category**: Content Marketing
**Difficulty**: Intermediate
**Estimated Tokens**: 600-800
**Version**: 1.0.0

## Description

Identify humorous commentary, witty observations, and entertaining personality moments perfect for social media. This prompt helps streamers find their funniest moments for viral content creation.

## The Prompt

```text
Please analyze this gaming stream transcript and extract the funniest commentary and entertaining personality moments.

STREAM TRANSCRIPT: [Paste your gaming stream transcript here]

Find these types of funny content:

## SPONTANEOUS HUMOR
- Unexpected funny observations about the game
- Witty responses to in-game situations
- Spontaneous jokes or commentary
- Funny reactions to game mechanics or glitches

## CHAT INTERACTIONS
- Humorous responses to viewer comments
- Funny reading of usernames, donations, or messages
- Interactive jokes with the community
- Entertaining responses to trolls or funny questions

## PERSONALITY MOMENTS
- Self-deprecating humor about gameplay mistakes
- Funny personal stories shared during gameplay
- Entertaining rants or passionate opinions
- Humorous character voices or impressions

## RELATABLE CONTENT
- Comments that resonate with general audiences
- Gaming frustrations that non-gamers understand
- Universal experiences told in a funny way
- Life observations made during gameplay

For each funny moment, provide:
- **Timestamp**: Exact start and end time
- **Humor Type**: Observational, self-deprecating, interactive, etc.
- **Standalone Value**: Whether it's funny without game context
- **Social Platform**: Best suited for TikTok/Twitter/Instagram
- **Caption Suggestion**: Engaging social media caption
- **Hashtag Ideas**: Relevant hashtags for discovery

Focus on content that showcases your personality and humor style rather than just gaming skill.
```

## Best Practices

**Personality over performance:** Prioritize funny commentary over impressive gameplay for broader appeal.

**Universal humor:** Focus on moments that work for both gaming and general audiences.

**Authenticity matters:** Genuine, spontaneous humor performs better than forced jokes.

**Context consideration:** Note which moments need gaming knowledge versus universal appeal.

## Use Cases

- **Comedy compilation videos** - Gather funniest moments from multiple streams
- **TikTok content creation** - Find short, punchy funny moments
- **Twitter clip sharing** - Identify quotable gaming humor
- **Instagram Reels** - Create personality-focused content
- **Community engagement** - Share relatable gaming experiences
- **Brand building** - Showcase humor style and personality
