## Description

Convert team standup transcripts into structured async communication that allows distributed teammates to contribute asynchronously. Ideal for remote teams spanning multiple timezones who need to coordinate work without requiring everyone online simultaneously.

## Best Practices

**Structure for scanning:** Distributed team members often need to quickly find relevant information. Use consistent formatting and clear headers.

**Include timezone conversions:** Convert all mentioned times to multiple timezones used by your team (e.g., PST, EST, GMT, IST).

**Highlight handoff opportunities:** Identify work that can be passed between team members in different timezones to maximize productivity.

**Separate urgent from routine:** Clearly distinguish items needing immediate attention from standard updates.

## Use Cases

- **Daily standups for remote teams** - Transform synchronous standups into async-friendly documentation
- **Sprint planning updates** - Keep distributed Agile teams aligned on sprint progress
- **Project team coordination** - Document daily progress for stakeholders and team members
- **Follow-the-sun workflows** - Enable seamless handoffs between teams in different regions
- **Manager briefings** - Provide leadership with structured team status without requiring attendance
