**Category**: Content Marketing

## Description

Transform meeting transcripts into comprehensive summaries optimized for distributed team members who couldn't attend. Perfect for async communication workflows where team members across timezones need to stay informed without watching full recordings.

```text

[Specific decisions with context and rationale]

**ACTION ITEMS**
[Owner name, specific task, deadline, dependencies]

**DISCUSSION CONTEXT**
[Background information, assumptions, alternatives considered]

[Specific questions requiring input from team members not present]
```

## Best Practices

**Include timezone context:** Always note participant timezones to help async team members understand when decisions were made and plan their responses accordingly.

**Prioritize actionable items:** Lead with decisions and action items since these require the most immediate attention from absent team members.

**Preserve discussion context:** Include the reasoning behind decisions so team members can provide informed input even without attending.

**Flag urgent items clearly:** Mark any time-sensitive items that require response within 24-48 hours.

## Use Cases

- **Daily standups for global teams** - Keep distributed teammates aligned without requiring everyone to attend live
- **Project status meetings** - Document progress updates for stakeholders in different timezones
- **Decision-making sessions** - Capture context for team members to review and provide async input
- **Client meetings** - Share outcomes with internal teams who couldn't attend
- **All-hands meetings** - Create searchable summaries for employees who missed company announcements
