# Tutorial and Educational Segment Identifier

**Category**: Content Marketing
**Difficulty**: Intermediate
**Estimated Tokens**: 700-1000
**Version**: 1.0.0

## Description

Find moments where you explain strategies, teach mechanics, or provide valuable gaming knowledge. This prompt helps streamers identify educational content perfect for tutorial videos and helpful guides.

## The Prompt

```text
Please analyze this gaming stream transcript and identify educational moments that could become tutorial content or helpful guides for other players.

STREAM TRANSCRIPT: [Paste your gaming stream transcript here]

Look for these educational opportunities:

## STRATEGY EXPLANATIONS
- Game mechanics tutorials or explanations
- Strategic decision-making processes
- Map knowledge, positioning, or timing advice
- Character/weapon/item optimization discussions

## TEACHING MOMENTS
- Responses to "how do you..." questions from chat
- Demonstration of specific techniques or skills
- Common mistake identification and correction
- Pro tips shared during gameplay

## ANALYTICAL CONTENT
- Meta discussions about game balance or updates
- Comparison of different strategies or builds
- Analysis of opponent behavior or tactics
- Game review or critique segments

## BEGINNER-FRIENDLY CONTENT
- Basic concept explanations that assume no prior knowledge
- Step-by-step process breakdowns
- "Why" explanations behind strategic choices
- Encouragement and mindset advice for improving players

For each educational segment, provide:
- **Timestamp**: When the explanation begins and ends
- **Skill Level**: Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced target audience
- **Completeness**: Whether explanation is self-contained or needs context
- **Tutorial Potential**: Standalone video/guide possibility
- **Search Keywords**: What players would search to find this content
- **Title Suggestion**: Educational video title for YouTube/TikTok

Prioritize content that helps other players improve and demonstrates your expertise in an accessible way.
```

## Best Practices

**Clear explanations:** Break complex strategies into simple, understandable steps.

**Visual context:** Note when visual demonstration enhances the educational value.

**Skill progression:** Organize content from basic to advanced concepts.

**Practical application:** Focus on tips that viewers can immediately implement.

## Use Cases

- **Tutorial video creation** - Turn stream segments into dedicated how-to guides
- **Educational content series** - Build reputation as knowledgeable instructor
- **Beginner-friendly guides** - Help new players learn game fundamentals
- **Advanced strategy content** - Share high-level insights for experienced players
- **Community building** - Establish yourself as helpful resource for improvement
- **SEO content creation** - Target "how to" search queries for discoverability

## Advanced Techniques

**Skill level targeting:** Create content for specific audiences:
```text
Additionally, categorize by learning objective:
- Foundational: Core concepts every player needs
- Intermediate: Building on basic skills
- Advanced: High-level strategy and optimization
- Specialized: Niche techniques for specific situations
```

**Series potential identification:** Group related content:
```text
For each educational moment, consider series potential:
- Part of broader topic that needs multiple videos
- Foundation for advanced concepts to cover later
- Common question that generates related questions
- Trending topic that audience wants more coverage of
```

## Integration Tips

**Content planning:** Use educational moments to plan future stream topics and tutorial series.

**Community engagement:** Ask chat which tutorials they'd like to see based on identified moments.

**Cross-platform promotion:** Share tutorial clips across all social media platforms to establish teaching reputation.
