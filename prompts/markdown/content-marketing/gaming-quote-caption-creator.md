## Description

Extract quotable moments and create engaging captions for social media posts across all platforms. This prompt identifies memorable statements and transforms them into shareable content.

## The Prompt

```text
Please analyze this gaming stream transcript and extract quotable moments that work well for social media posts, memes, and community engagement.

STREAM TRANSCRIPT: [Paste your gaming stream transcript here]

Create different types of social media content:

## QUOTABLE MOMENTS
Find statements that are:
- **Relatable**: Thoughts other gamers have but haven't expressed well
- **Funny**: Humorous observations that work out of context
- **Inspirational**: Motivational thoughts about gaming, improvement, or perseverance
- **Controversial**: Opinions that will spark friendly debate and engagement

## MEME POTENTIAL
Identify moments that could become:
- **Reaction Images**: Expressions that others could use to respond
- **Format Templates**: Statements that could become meme formats
- **Community Inside Jokes**: References that your audience could adopt
- **Viral Phrases**: Catchphrases that could spread beyond your community

## INSTAGRAM CAPTIONS
For each quote, create:
- **Hook**: Opening line that stops scrolling
- **Context**: Brief setup without over-explaining
- **Engagement Question**: Question that encourages comments
- **Hashtag Strategy**: Mix of trending and niche gaming hashtags
- **Call to Action**: Gentle encouragement to follow or engage

## TWITTER CONTENT
For each quote, create:
- **Standalone Tweet**: Quote that works without additional context
- **Thread Starter**: Opinion that could begin a discussion thread
- **Reply Value**: Statement that invites thoughtful responses
- **Retweet Factor**: Why other gamers would want to share this
- **Community Engagement**: How this connects with gaming culture

## TikTok TEXT OVERLAYS
For each quote, suggest:
- **Opening Text**: Attention-grabbing statement for video start
- **Build-Up Text**: How to create anticipation before the quote
- **Punchline Delivery**: Optimal timing for maximum impact
- **Follow-Up Text**: How to extend engagement after the quote
- **Hashtag Integration**: How to make content discoverable

Please focus on authentic moments that showcase personality while being entertaining and shareable across gaming communities.
```

**Engagement optimization:** Create captions that encourage interaction and discussion.
